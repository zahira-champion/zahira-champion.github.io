Foundations Reflection

Core Learning

Through the core learning, there are 3 things that stood out to me. The first one was properly learning how to use emotional intelligence. I had heard/read about it before but never put It into practise in my everyday life. I found learning about neuroplasticity super useful and how amazing the brain can be, how it can stretch and grow and take on new information. Even with this, I find myself lacking the confidence in myself to learn something when I’m stuck or blocked even though I know the brain is capable of so many wonderful things. I found growth mindset quite interesting and useful. I already seemed to have an understanding of growth mindset without realising what it actually meant. There are a couple of people around me often who use growth mindset daily so I am using that to help me further my daily life not just while studying.

Values, Empathy and Self Awareness in Programming

Being able to be a team player is a huge part of programming but to do that you need an understanding of values, empathy and self awareness. These 3 things impact not just yourself but your coworkers and how they work with you.

Core Learning Surprises

I am quite surprised at how much core material we did but I can see why it is helpful. Understanding how the brain works and how you learn has quite an impact on your growth. In saying that I did feel like it was quite a lot of time to put into learning about it as I got quite behind in the tech side of things.

Core Learning Challenges

Learning about my values and identity was the hardest part, especially talking about my identity and how your upbringing has an effect on your choices.

EDA and Core Learning

I’m not sure I would say studying core was a waste of time but I do think that the amount of time put into it could have been less. The tech part is very time consuming plus you get stuck quite a lot and I did not stick to the time boxes at all really. I would have felt I was making more progress faster if I hadn’t focused on core so much.
